What is Random flag?

Welcome to all you need to know about random flag. Random flag is the #1 Trivia platform where users can answer questions on any topic of their choice from History to Movies, Sports and so on, it also give users access to host their own questions. Gain points⭐ and flags🚩 on each correct answers and when lot of users interact on your questions.


Guide

1. Every correct answer is +1 point⭐.

2. Every quiz-session carries 10 questions.

3. Points are not counted when you Leave a quiz-session before it ends.

4. Some sections of these game will remain locked until you reach level Intermediate🥉.


Game levels

Levels depends on the game flags🚩, each time a player earns a flag his level upgrades. You earn a flag🚩each time you accumulate a total of 20 points⭐ from answering questions.

Levels Meter

Beginner🚀(0-19⭐, 0🚩)

Intermediate🥉(20-39⭐, 1🚩)

Advanced🥈(40-59⭐, 2🚩)

Veteran🥇(60-79⭐, 3🚩)

Professional🏆(80-99⭐, 4🚩)
